# ⚡ Eliflar — Quick Guide

> **Draw on images. Live. Instantly.**
> This guide covers everything you need to start in under 2 minutes.

---
## Create narrated tutorial videos without video editing!
## 🎛️ The Indicator (top-left)

The small badge in the top-left corner is your control center.
It shows: **image number · active color · active mode · line thickness**

| Gesture | Action |
|---|---|
| 👆 Single tap | Cycle to the next drawing color |
| 👆👆 Double tap *(quick)* | Switch to the next image — smooth crossfade |
| ✋ Long press *(0.6 s)* | Cycle line thickness: `10 → 30 → 10 → 50 px` |

---

## 🖊️ Drawing on screen

Touch and drag anywhere on the image to draw.
Marks **auto-fade** after a few seconds — that is the Flare effect.

---

## 🗂️ Left strip — Drawing modes

Tap the **left edge** of the screen to change mode.
The strip is split into **5 vertical zones** (top to bottom):

| Zone | Icon | Mode |
|---|---|---|
| Zone 1 — Top | ✨ | **Dust / Spray** — scattered dots around your finger |
| Zone 2 | ⬛ | **Rectangle** — drag to draw |
| Zone 3 — Middle | ⭕ | **Ellipse** — drag to draw |
| Zone 4 | 📏 | **Straight Line** — with a dot at the endpoint |
| Zone 5 — Bottom | 🔒 | **Freeze** — marks stay permanently *(tap again to clear)* |

> **Default mode:** 🖊️ Flare Line — freehand, auto-fading marks.
> Changing color or thickness always resets back to Flare Line.

---

## 💡 Quick tips

- Draw **fast circular strokes** for the best light-trail effect
- Use **🔒 Freeze** to hold a drawing in place while you speak
- **Double-tap** the indicator any time to move to the next image
